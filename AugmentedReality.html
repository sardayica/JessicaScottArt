Welcome In (2025)

Made with Blender, 3D Sketchfab models, and Niantic 8th Wall

This is an augmented reality experience with three different interactions to explore, all pertaining to dreams. There is a peaceful dream, which has the themes of whimsy and relaxation. There is a nightmare, depicting darker themes and uncomfortability. Finally there is a confusing dream, which shows an odd assortment of things and settings. This piece is a conversation between the act of dreaming and augmented reality, highlighting the inherent otherworldly nature they both possess.
